The Way of the Blade

Harnessing the honor of the Warrior Samurai you draw your Katana and face the masive Khan standing before you.

The Khan is unwavering and watches you as you stand with an iron pose. He waits for you to make your move.

With a scream you charge at the Khan; using all the skills you learned as a child of honor.

The Khan suddenly swings a club at you, laced with poison tips.

The Khan will fight a dirty battle. How will you fight?
