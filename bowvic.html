An Honorable Shot

With the Mongolian Khan pushing your location you stand your ground with your bow strung taunt.

Sweat drips down your chin as you stand still as a board while the Khan charges at you like a bull.

As the Khan swings his club the sweat falls and in one smooth motion you fire off a volley of 3 arrows.

Striking all of the joints between the centimer gaps between his armor plates you manage to defeat him.

A truely honorable shot for a Samurai of your caliber.
